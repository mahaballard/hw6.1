Homework 6: Census data choropleth

In this assignment, I created a choropleth of the percentage of households in each county in California that earn an income between $75,000 and $99,9999 in 2019. I chose this variable because the median income in CA in 2019 was approximately $81,000, give or take. This map is in the Lambert Conformal projection EPSG: 102009.
